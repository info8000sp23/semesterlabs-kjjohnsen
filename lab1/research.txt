My lab focuses on the design, application, and evaluation of human-computer interfaces, especially those that feature virtual and augmented reality technologies. 

Students in the lab are working on:

* AR environments to study learning through eye and pen tracking - Brook Bowers
* Virtual Pets to promote healthy behaviors in children - Catherine Ball
* Enhancing robotic interfaces with AR and VR - Andrew Rukangu
* Tracking human facial expressions in mobile VR - Yulong Wang
* Improving gestural navigation in collaborative VR worlds - Anton Franzluebbers
